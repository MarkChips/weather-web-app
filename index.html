To start a new game, begin by searching for a location.



Instructions on how to play:

Sun, Clouds, Rain is a variant of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

  1. The computer will randomly select a weather type.
  2. The User must search for a location. The weather of today and the next five days will be their play move.
  3. The graph below determines which weather is stronger. Whoever wins the most weather comparisons out of six is the winner.
  4. To start the next round, simply search for your next location.
  5. Wildcard: Thunderstorms will win no matter what
  • sun > rain > clouds > sun
  • sun = clear sky or few clouds
  • clouds = scattered clouds, broken clouds, mist
  • rain = shower rain, rain, thunderstorm, snow
